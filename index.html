The Woods

Behind the neighborhood, past the last backyard fence, the trees started. We went in every day after school. We knew every trail.

The Clearing

A hidden field inside the woods. Tall grass, warm light flooding in. No canopy for the first time in a mile.

The Creek

A valley cut through the middle of everything. Water over flat stones, cold even in August.

The Large Rock

A single boulder at the forest edge. High enough to see over the treeline, into the field beyond.

The Treehouse

Boards nailed into the old oak. Not level. Not weatherproof. Ours.
